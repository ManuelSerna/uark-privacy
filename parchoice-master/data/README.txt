This folder contains training and test files from two blog authors in the Blog Authorship Corpus, renamed "Alice and "Bob".

The Blog Authorship Corpus is described in:
J. Schler, M. Koppel, S. Argamon and J. Pennebaker (2006). Effects of Age and Gender on Blogging. In Proceedings of 2006 AAAI Spring Symposium on Computational Approaches for Analyzing Weblogs.
URL: http://www.cs.biu.ac.il/~schlerj/schler_springsymp06.pdf

The Blog Authorship Corpus can be downloaded from:
https://u.cs.biu.ac.il/~koppel/BlogCorpus.htm
